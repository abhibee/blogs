Categories of Security in India

There are 6 different levels of security in India, they are:

I. SPG Level

It is the highest level of security in India.

  • The Special Protection Group (SPG) was raised in 1985 with the intention to provide proximate security cover to the Prime Minister, former Prime Ministers and their immediate family members.
  • They are very secretive and very little is known about them.
  • They work with both state police and foreign police to provide protection to the prime minister.
  • The number of SPG commandos is about 3000 but the actual number is strictly confidential.
  • SPG officers are ingrained in high leadership qualities, professionalism, knowledge of proximate security and inculcate the culture of leading from the front.
  • Since inception of SPG, the saga of bravery is very well established. SPG officers have been so far awarded by 01 Shaurya Chakra, 43 President’s Police Medals for distinguished Service and 319 Police Medals for Meritorious Service.
  • SPG officers are trained to offer, if necessary, the supreme sacrifice to ensure that the sacred task assigned to the organization by the Government is fulfilled at all costs.

II. Z+ Level

Z+ is the second highest category security in India.

  • It offers a security cover of 55 personnel, including 10+ NSG Commandos + Police Personnel.
  • Each commando is an expert in martial arts and unarmed combat.
  • Z+ security is provided by the NSG commandos equipped with sophisticated MP5 guns, and modern communication gadgets.
  • Rahul Gandhi, Amit Shah, Rajnath Singh,Yogi Adityanath, Priyanka Gandhi and N. Chandrababu Naidu are entitled to Z+ security.

III. Z level

This is the third highest level of security.

  • Z category provides a security cover of 22 personnel, including 4 or 5 NSG Commandos + Police Personnel.
  • It is provided by the Delhi police or the ITBP or CRPF personnel along with one escort car.
  • Z security is provided to Yoga Guru Ramdev and several actors.

IV. Y+ level

This is the fourth highest level of security

  • Y+ level provides a security cover of a CRPF commando and four constables + Six personal security officers (PSOs) are deployed with the protectee on a rotational basis in three shifts.
  • This means that two PSOs are with the protectee at all times
  • Kangana Ranaut was provided with Y+ level security in September last year.

V. Y level

  • This is the fifth highest level of security.
  • Y category has a security cover of 11 personnel, including 1 or 2 Commandos + Police Personnel.
  • It offers two personal security officers (PSOs).
  • There are a considerable number of people who get this category of protection in India.

VI. X level

This is the sixth level of security.

  • X category has a security cover of 2 personnel (No Commando, Only Armed Police Personnel).
  • It is provided by one PSO (personal security officer). Again, a considerable number of people get this category of protection in India.
