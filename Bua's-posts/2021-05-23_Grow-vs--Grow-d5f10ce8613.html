Grow vs. Grow

There is a difference between just growing up and growing as a person. You may just Grow with time which refers to physical growth and you may also grow as a person which means you learn everyday to be a better being and never stop improving yourself. Time will never stop for anyone and the physical growth is inevitable. We don’t have control over that. But if anyone grows or not grows mentally, emotionally and spiritually is a personal choice.

Whenever one meets a very old friend they are mostly greeted as you have not changed at all. It is because people picture you the way they have known you forever. In my case it’s not true I have changed a lot and I change everyday. So many inputs are available on daily basis. It’s my choice I want to imbibe it in me to become a better person or not. I try to change myself a little everyday except few things which I don’t want to change at all.

Human has the ability to unlearn and learn things which is a superpower which God has bestowed upon us. We need to unlearn things which are not right and learn better things. Learning and unlearning are both part of growing up as a person.

  • The four aspects where a person can work upon everyday are
    1. Physical development ( Physical Quotient):- one must learn how to have a healthy lifestyle. Our body is the home of our soul and we must grow healthier to grow happier.
    2. Mental development( Intelligence Quotient):- mental well-being is the key to overall growth. It is said that happiness is a state of mind. We must endeavour for the growth of our mind by feeding it with positive inputs.
    3. Emotional Development ( Emoitional Quotient ):- one must grow emotionally strong. It is very important for the journey called life to be emotionally stable. One must learn to forgive fast and embrace everyone and be at peace with themselves.
    4. Spiritual development ( Spiritual quotient):- this is another aspect where growth is important. Spirituality gives trust and faith and also the cleansing of soul. One must grow far from superstitions and towards the inner consciousness.

It is ok to make mistakes. But self realisation is very important. That is the only way one can grow .Everyone is allowed to change irrespective of their past. If everyone grow for betterment, world will be more beautiful. One needs to step out of their comfort zone slowly and steadily to grow as a person.

In order to bloom one needs to grow.
