I am OK

Everyone has an inner protector who helps a person sail through any problem also helps a person to distinguish right from wrong. It has many names like faith,self conscience,or may be The God himself. It’s my belief that God is there in every being. If someone loves me or is good to me may be the God within them is happy with me because of some good deed of mine anywhere anytime. If someone is not being nice to me or is angry with me, may be the God inside them is not happy with me for some mistakes I made somewhere sometime, knowingly unknowingly. Sometimes strangers give very nice positive vibes when you want to interact with them or are comfortable with them being around, and sometimes even strangers give very negetive vibes which make me wonder why?

I tell myself that is OK ,I am OK. Meditation is very important to keep your inner protector alive or even one can pray for sometime in a day with full faith that also gives a similar vibe as meditation. Meditation helps to calm the outer noise and activate the inner calmness. I have a habit of saying prayer just before sleeping in night since I was a child. This helps my mind relax and calm. Having a calm mind is very important for living a fulfilling and a happy life.

That’s my faith.
