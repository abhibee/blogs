Unspoken

Body language is a form of non verbal communication by which our gestures, expressions,and body position and movement convey unspoken messages. Ideally,the message expressed by body language should match the verbal message. However,if these two contradict,then you are sending out confusing signals to other people.

This reminds me of the parents teachers meet of my son from the preschool days to around the primary school days. The teachers were very confused as his body language always made them think that he is busy in something else but when they asked him questions about what they were teaching, he was promt to reply. I used to tell them that he visualises everything he hear so it’s not that he is not paying attention, only his body language says otherwise. But as he has grown now he is an attentive and a teacher’s delight kind of kid.

This takes me to think what can be a positive body language. When we meet someone we must take care to use right body language, that gives a very good impression on them. Some indications of positive body language are as follows:-

  • Make eye contact
  • Hold your head level
  • Keep your chin up
  • Sit straight without slouching
  • Stand straight with both feet on the floor
  • Allow a comfort space around you when conversing with others
  • Walk with grace and ease
  • Smile often
    It is nice if our body language conveys the same thing as we want to convey or sometimes it is the cause of some unpleasantness. One needs to be alert while speaking to anyone, so that they can discourse them in the right manner.
