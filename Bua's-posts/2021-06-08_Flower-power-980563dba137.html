Flower power

I love to go for walk anytime in the day and if the walk is in midst of nature, what better can I ask for. I feel some sort of connection with different kinds of flowers all around. I always get excited to see how flowers can have so many different shades , different shapes , different size , different fragrance. I love if someone gifts me flowers and I find flower decoration indeed very beautiful. Since my kids were very young they used to bring flowers which had fallen from the plants for me , that was such an emotional gesture and their way of showing love for their mother. Every season brings different varieties of flowers and I love to take pictures while walking. I have passed this to my kids too they also keep noticing different flowers all around.

Anyone who receives flower as gift express their emotions in varied ways. It brings a smile on the face of recipient irrespective of their age. It has been the most traditional gift one can give to someone. It is such a nice way to show different emotions like gratitude, appreciation,recognition,respect and also love. It can uplift the mood of anyone who is sad or depressed, troubled or agitated. They can induce a more positive outlook on life, boosting energy and happiness. Nature provides us with a simple way to improve emotional health . The presence of flowers triggers happy emotions, heightens feelings of life satisfaction and affects social behavior in a positive manner far beyond what is normally believed.

I like to decorate my house with flowers and also keep different seasonal flowering plants in the balcony garden. It gives a very happy feeling whenever I see them bloom. It’s our tradition to offer flowers to God while doing pooja. No pooja is complete without flowers. We offer to Him what He has given to us. It is amazing to see how nature has given us so many gifts and ways to keep ourselves happy and healthy. Nature is God’s gift to us and we must try to preserve the beauty of nature in any way we can.
