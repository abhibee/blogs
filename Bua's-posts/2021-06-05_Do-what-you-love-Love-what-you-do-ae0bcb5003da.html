Do what you love
Love what you do

One must choose to do the things they love to do. That way one can enjoy their work and it won’t be tedious at all. That gives immense satisfaction. Also if it’s something you choose out of your passion you will never complaint. Satisfaction is not always in monetary terms , but satisfaction that you are doing the right thing you are meant for is of another level.

Studies show that the chances of success increases if a person chooses to do something which they are fascinated with rather than one which he doing out of compulsion. The work which you choose out of interest will be more fulfilling. If you are passionate about your job, you are likely to take an active interest in learning every aspect of it, thus being the best in it. This is not only true for the occupation or business you are in but for everything you do in life. And if you become the best in doing your work you can inspire others to follow suit too. This is a universal truth.

Now let’s see the other scenario. Shall we not love what we do. It is not possible that everyone does the things which they love to do. There are larger number of people who do things which are not their passion, but they do everything in the best possible way and succeed too. That is because they love what they do. If one has no choice and which is the larger possibility, then one must not start feel sorry about the situation, that is the negative approach to life. If someone cribs about what they are doing , doesn’t mean that they won’t have to do it. They still will have to do it, like it or not. So the positive approach will be to start liking what they are doing, that way they will start enjoying the same. The most important gift given to humans is adaptability. So one needs to keep their minds open and try to adapt to the situation with positive attitude. And then the cycle repeats. Once you start loving what you are doing, happiness will flow, success will follow.

It is important to choose to do what you love but if it is not possible then one must start loving what they do. Keeping a positive approach in life can help a person live a happy and fulfilling life.
