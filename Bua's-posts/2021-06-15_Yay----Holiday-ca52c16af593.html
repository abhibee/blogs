Yay!!! Holiday

Be it a breezy beach holiday or plush green cold hill station or a silent jungle, one needs a change from the regular routine work,home, surroundings. It is very important to take sometime out and head for a vacation. It’s such a carefree time when we are on vacation and it has a loads of benefits too.

When we visit a different place we can understand the topography culture people of that place very closely. One can always know such details if they read about it, but being there and feeling all is very different. Thus a person grows a little more empathetic to other cultures and become more inclusive.

Going on a vacation with family or friends strengthen the bond between us. As we get to spend all the time together without bothering about work etc. It’s such amazing time where we create beautiful memories. Clicking lot of pictures and revisiting them later brings back the feeling of being there. It’s such a happy thing to do.

It reduces the stress level of people who are busy in there daily lives. It gives an opportunity to them to stop a little and reflect on their lives. It is crucial to pause for sometime and vacation gives the much needed time to relax and rejuvenate.

There are many health benefits of taking vacations too. This is the best way to improve health in the most enjoyable way. Also the productivity of a person increases when they return from a relaxing vacation. People also start appreciating their home and work more after returning from a vacation. When people visit different places their creativity also increases. When one sees different things in life they try to incorporate few in their lives too. That is how it is beneficial for growing as a person too.

In the end, afterall what is the purpose of earning and working so much if you don’t enjoy your life. When one plans for a vacation there is something to look forward to. I have experienced that even before going for a vacation I start feeling happy just by preparing for it. Before every vacation we plan what all we will do where we will stay and the best part is I head shopping as I love to wear everything new when on vacation. It’s like a festival for me.

For quite sometime we are not able to go for a vacation because of covid situation. But I am positive that we will soon plan a safe and fun filled vacation. I am eagerly looking forward to it.
