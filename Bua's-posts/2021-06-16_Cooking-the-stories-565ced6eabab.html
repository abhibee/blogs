Cooking the stories

Writing blogs has become like cooking for me. Lot of time goes into deciding what to write just like everyday I keep thinking what to cook. But once I decide it is not that time taking.

This takes me to the question can writing be compared to cooking in any other ways too. After giving little thought, I concluded yes it can be, let me tell you how.

Cooking is delightful when there is lots of variety. No body likes to cook or to that matter eat the same thing everyday. Similarly writing blogs also needs lot of varied topics to make writings compelling for everyone. In this way writing can be compared to cooking.

Cooked food looks delectable when it is presented in a beautiful manner. Presentation somehow enhances the taste of the dishes. Similarly the presentation of the writings is also very important. If an article is presented beautifully, the readers find it riveting. This way also writing can be compared to cooking.

Experimenting with food is something which brings newness to the dishes. We try to put different flavours which makes the dishes look fresh and intersting. Similarly when writing an article we try to use new words and vocabulary which brings a freshness to the article thus making it engrossing.

So in many ways writing can be compared to cooking. And like to master any dish one needs time and practice, the skill of writing articles is also refined by practice over time.
