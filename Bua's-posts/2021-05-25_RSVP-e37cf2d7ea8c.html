RSVP

Language is powerful. If one knows how to use proper language,one can easily win hearts and thereby winning whatever they seek. A language is given to us from birth but it’s our choice if we want to learn more and more languages. Learning more than one languages has many benefits.

  • Firstly research has shown how speaking more than one language help improve memory. One can have better problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, enhanced concentration, ability to multitask, and better listening skills.
  • There are many different cultures around the world and learning the languages help understanding different cultures better. Greater understanding can promote greater tolerance, empathy and acceptance for others.
  • It boosts confidence of a person.
  • One can visit different places all over the world and with the knowledge of local language one can mingle with local people and can understand the place better.
  • It can give a boost to the career prospects as the one who is polylingual is supposed to have a better decision making abilities.

But even if one is not interested in learning many languages they can for sure learn one or two, but mastering those is important. One of the most important aspect for learning any language is
RSVP:- Reading, spelling, vocabulary and pronunciation
Reading helps in understanding how to use the language. We can understand how to and when to use particular words, sentences,punctuations etc.

Spelling is very important to make an impression. If you make mistakes in spelling you are not taken seriously by anyone.

Vocabulary is utmost important as different words can be used to describe different feelings,things etc. If you know the actual word you will always have an upper hand while using the language.

Pronounciation is very critical as a wrong pronounciation can lead to different meanings and thus one looses the audience.
By mastering the RSVP one can easily convey what he is thinking and it is important to reach ad idem ie meeting of minds.

People who have excellent communication skills owe it to their command over language. Many people who are fluent in one language can use their skill to their advantage. Actually language is a skill which is not God gifted but is a gift one chooses for themselves.

Language is powerful. It does not just describe reality. It creates the reality it describes.
