What music means to me .

I love Music. I am not a singer. I appreciate good music. Music helps me destress .

Music represents emotions, good times, bad times and messages from your inner self.

Music is a way to express yourself, keep you company while you’re alone, and always give you something to do.”

I love to listen to music. I relate music to events in my life..

We went to US ( before my marriage) when Amit was doing MBA . We got a new cassette by Sonu Nigam . Song https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=6vMWoJFgUYI

Chui Mui si tum lagti ho . We heard this song in car continuously. Even today when this song play it reminds us of our trip .

Wada raha sanam was played in Saraswati puja in our childhood home lake gardens. That song reminds me , my brother and sister of our lake gardens days till today.

Khuda jana from Bachna ae hasino .. reminds me of Atlanta days 2008 when I got married.

Music is an emotion. My morning became lively if I hear to good music. Morning walks became enjoyable with music. Evening when you just sit and listen to good songs becomes special.
