Golden Emotions

Today I will write about celebrations . Today is my mom and dad’s 50 th wedding anniversary. It is a special day for us and we had so many plans. We wanted to have a grand celebration. Invite everyone and recreate 20 th May 1971 again. My mother is always excited about her anniversary and makes plans one month in advance. Goes for shopping and makes sure she gets the best gift from my papa.

Today my mom didn’t even ask for a gift. She said her best celebration is that her family is healthy and smiling. We can celebrate every day. Celebrate the laughter, celebrate the happiness, celebrate the conversation with near and dear, celebrate the care you get.

Count your blessings and you will be the happiest person on earth.

I am celebrating the smile on my parent's faces. I am celebrating they are just a call away for me. I am celebrating that God has blessed me with such lovely parents which tells me I am god’s favorite child.

By Payal Sharma on May 20, 2021.
