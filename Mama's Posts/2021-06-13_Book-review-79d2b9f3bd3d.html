Book review

I started reading this book 2–3 weeks back. When my friend Neha told me to read and my sister and brother also bought this book.

We all started to read the book. As the name suggests you can heal your life . So true. No one in the world can help you if you don’t want to help yourself. It is inside you .

The book is about Thoughts. How your thoughts have power to heal you or destroy you. I was very inspired by Louise hay’s life story. She cured her Cancer only through healing and affirmation . No medicine No chemotherapy. I felt this is awesome. If she can heal her cancer.. I can heal small aches and pains in my body. Our thoughts reflect on our body. Skin disease are caused due to-our thoughts, anger , frustration, anxiety .

For me this book is my best friend now . I have to keep reading it everyday. Mind also needs daily training like our body.

I recommend this book to all who is looking for positive change in life . Spread good vibes.

By Payal Sharma on June 13, 2021.
