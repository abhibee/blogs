What is memories in life?

Memory is our ability to encode, store, retain and subsequently recall information and past experiences in the human brain. It can be thought of in general terms as the use of past experience to affect or influence current behaviour.

What is memories in life?

Memories are very essential in our lives because they allow us to grow and learn to be a better person. Our recollections can teach us very important life lessons, demonstrate skills and abilities and can make us feel happy and entertained.

Making memories in life is so important. There are few incidents in life which we always remember and some memories becomes turning point in our life . Good or bad we learn from each and every incident and grow .

Thanks to today ‘s generation phones we can capture each and every moment. We can refresh our memories anytime

When I got married Abhishek got his new Camera. He used to click each and every moment pics . Be it with our luggage, getting inside the flight. Each moment. I used to think why so many pics but when I see them today I can live each and every moment. Each moment is so fresh in my mind. Thanks to Abhishek.

When I was expecting Sangini I wrote a diary for 9 months. My pregnancy journey. Now when I read the diary each memory comes in front of my eyes . Otherwise I would not have remembered anything.I understand my thought process then and now .

We make memories each moment. We have 2 kinds of memory Short term memory ( STM ) and Long term memory ( LTM) .

Live life fully and make beautiful memories. Life is gods gift. It is precious.
