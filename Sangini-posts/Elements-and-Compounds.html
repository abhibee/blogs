Elements and Compounds and their game

Elements are made of only 1 kind of atom

There are many of them like cerium and helium

96% of your body are made of elements

Just 4 that stick together, just like cement

There is Hydrogen and Nitrogen

That can be filled in a balloon

Then there is carbon and oxygen

And they can’t be stolen by any goon!

Compounds are made of different kinds of atoms, it can be 2 or more

And one of them is the water that we pour!

Another is salt but there is another name for it too!

If you want to be fancy, you could call it Sodium chloride too!

The water and salt teamed up and challenged the elements to a game

So the Oxegen and Nitrogen joined in, excepting the challenge for the game that spread fame

All the elements and compounds had collected to watch

And they collected on a ground that no human was ever going to touch

But now the problem was, they didn't know which game to play,

And they pondered “What game can you play in the middle of May”?

They decided on playing Kabaddi

And what a hilarious game it was too

The gases went right through the salt

And when the oxygen went right through the water, the spectators shouted “Boo!”

Everyone said we want to see something fair

So they started playing bowling and the spectators lined up in a square

The water sprayed with much force on the ball

It went for a strike and the water shouted “I am winning you all!”

The gases ran through the ball very hard

And the gases became tiered

So that's why it was decided to play no game at all

And they became friends and climbed a tree, not afraid they will fall

And then all the spectators came rushing to see

But then water fell down on all of them and they all screamed “Whee!”
