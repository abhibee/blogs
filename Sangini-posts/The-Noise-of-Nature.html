The Noise of Nature

My balcony is carpeted with artificial grass and me and my brother love to make tents on it in the balcony so one windy day we made one in the smaller balcony of our house. It was a small tent but very cozy and we enjoyed playing camping in it. Yesterday night, me and my brother stayed in the tent for half an hour. In the last ten minutes, we decided to listen to what was happening around us in the night. So we shut the balcony gate and lay down on the soft grass and fell silent. What I heard was amazing. I heard the crickets chirping and a few kinds of birds flying around. I wondered what they were. When I continued to listen, I heard the other nighttime insects flying around right in the tent!

The next morning, my brother was still sleeping. I decided to see how it was in the morning. so I went into the tent and lay down again. Now this time I heard so many kinds of birds, chirping and spreading a soft song to the waking world. It was a soothing sound and I almost fell asleep! Now I understand the synchronization of the noises of nature.
