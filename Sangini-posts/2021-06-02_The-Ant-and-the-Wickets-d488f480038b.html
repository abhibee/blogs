The Ant and the Wickets

(A match is going on with India bowling. India had taken 7 wickets and had to take 3 more to win.)

In the game of Cricket

There was an ant near the wickets

Next to the wicket was a hole

And next to it, the bowler never seemed to bowl

And now the ant came along

Humming a happy song

He went near the wicket and found the hole

And dug over there with his heart and soul

The wickets fell and made the batsman pout

Because then everyone began to shout “You are out!”

The next batsman came onto the field

The wind was getting stronger, so he gave himself a shield

The wind was also strong for the ant

So he decided to climb the batsman’s pant!

The bowler threw the ball at him

And the batsman looked at the ball, grim

But then he caught sight of the ant

And screamed “There is an ant on my pant”

In his panic, he knocked over the bails

And everyone shouted, “This batsman also fails!”

The third batsman came

And his batting had brought him fame

The other team players thought “He was going to win the match.

He will never allow a run-out or a catch!”

The batsman took his place and rubbed his leg on the ground

The ant meanwhile thought “There is yet a comfortable place to be found!”

He climbed up this batsman too

Thinking there was no silly thing this man could do!

He climbed and climbed, up to the face

Just as the bowled started to pick up pace

The ant went in and thought it would be comfortable in the nose

Just as the ball escaped the bowler’s pose

The batsman started sneezing and mistakenly hit the ball

And everyone looked to see where it would fall

It went right next to a fielder and he threw it back

And it struck the wickets with a clack

The batsman was not in the line

And to the team, it was fine!

They won the match and got the trophy

But never knew the last wickets were because of the Cheenti!

By Sangini Sharma on June 2, 2021.
