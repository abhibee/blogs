Knowledge Encyclopedia

The Knowledge Encyclopedia is the best encyclopedia I have read. It has 6 sections, the first one for Space, the second one for Earth, the third one of Nature, The fourth one for the Human Body, the fifth one for Science and the sixth one for History. 1 thing I like the most about this book is that it has labeled pictures for everything. Another thing I like is the picture timelines which make them very much more interesting. For example, this timeline of the first humans where there are so many pictures but I find most of them funny! :

So this is one of my favourite books and my 3 most favourite pages are:

  1. Robotics — the NAO robot

This is a robot that can walk, talk and respond. It can even dance!

2. The first towns

On this page, there is a whole picture that explained the first towns!

3. Roman Society

On this page, there is a photo and description of the roman callosum and I find that quite interesting!

So overall, I like this book very much and I rate it a 10/10
