Instruments

I have many instruments in my house with me

A drum, a guitar, and a keyboard whose keys count up to 63!

That is not a normal number for a keyboard

So the guitar teases it until everyone is bored!

Only the drum keeps everything in peace,

Trying to stop the arguments about the keyboard and his keys

Sometimes he is listened to, sometimes not

Because an argument between a guitar and a keyboard is hard to sort!

But one of them went like this

An argument that brought the drum bliss:

All the instruments were on the dining table

And next to them was the internet cable

Now the guitar jumped up and taunted the piano about his keys —

“Your keys are 63 so you are equal to a hundred babies!”

He stood and did a silly dance on the table

and slipped and fell on the internet cable

Over the side, he went, down, down, down

But the keyboard only watched, wearing a frown!

One of the guitar’s strings got stuck like a hook

But by the piano, no notice was took!

The drum, however, urged the piano to help

And the piano came over as he heard the guitar yelp

The drum told the keyboard what to do

“We need as much help from you!”

The piano tied the guitar string all around him, full

And then shouted “Pull”

The guitar came up slowly and jumped on the dining table

He said “Thank you drum and piano, but what a pity about that silly cable”

They saw the cable around the guitar, like a snake

And laugh and became friends and it was the best team you could make!

By Sangini Sharma on May 26, 2021.
