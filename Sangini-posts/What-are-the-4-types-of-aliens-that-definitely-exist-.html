What are the 4 types of aliens that definitely exist

  1. The Blop-Blop Alien

The Blop-Blop Alien will be green in colour originally but can change its colour. But the Alien can only change its colour to orange when it likes. His smile is evil but he is a good boy and helps everyone. He is from the planet Bonkeria and can only communicate with his antennas.

2. The SoggySnake Alien

The SoggySnake Alien is an Alien which is loose and soggy looking, and mover around like a snake. But it can grow legs and a body anytime it wants but it will still have its soggy skin. This is a very bad boy alien and likes to curl itself on anything’s legs, especially the gentle Moomaw animal of the planet Moozania. He also lives on the planet Moozania.

3. Moomaw Alien Animal

The Moomaw Alien is a gentle animal of the alien’s planet. It looks much like a cow except that her skin is green. She mostly eats sand and its most favourite food is a fire which sometimes springs upon the planet and instead of giving milk, she gives liquid gold!

4. The Roller Alien

The Roller Alien rolls about everywhere and cannot walk anywhere. It is red in colour and has 2 antennas that enable it to walk upside down.
