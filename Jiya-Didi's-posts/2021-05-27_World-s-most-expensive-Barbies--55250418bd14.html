World’s most expensive Barbies.

Every one has heard about Barbie dolls and today I will tell you some of the most expensive barbies in the world.

5 Most expensive Barbies:

Lorraine Schwartz Barbie

It’s high cost is due to her diamond encrusted belt, shoes and earrings. Dressed in black this barbie costs $25,000

Pink Diamond barbie

As the name suggests this barbie is covered in small rose and fuchsia diamonds. It is also matched with a faux pink fur coat. It’s cost is $15,000

The original barbie

This barbie is in one of the most expensive because it was one of the first barbie ever made. She does not adorn any fancy jewellery and one of the main reasons it’s expensive is because it’s rare. Its price is $27,450.

De Beers 40th anniversary Barbie

In collaboration with De Beers, Barbie was dressed in an orange and pink evening gown. Her belt was encrusted with 160 diamonds and white gold jewellery. This doll is worth $85,000. It’s a big jump but wait for the last one.

Finally, the most expensive barbie ever made:

Barbie by Stefano Canturi.

This doll is the most expensive barbie because, jewellery designer Stefano Canturi adorned this limited-edition doll with a rare emerald-cut 1 carat Australian pink diamond, and 3-carat white diamonds. Its total with is $302,000.
