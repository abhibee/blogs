Cookie Cake

Can’t decide between cookie or cake? this is for you!

These cookie cakes are soft like cake from inside and crunchy like cookies on the outside! The best companion to eat with milk or just eat it like that!

Ingredients:

  • All purpose Flour — ¾ cup
  • Coco powder, unsweetened — ¼ cup
  • Sugar — ½ cup
  • Baking Soda — ¼ tsp
  • Butter salted — ¼ cup, room temperature
  • Milk — 3 tbsp

Procedure:

  1. Sieve flour, coco powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl. Mix and set aside.
  2. Take another deep bowl and add butter, sugar and milk in it.
  3. Whip it with electric whisk for about 2–3 minutes on high, till it becomes creamy and light.
  4. Slowly add and mix the flour mixture to this butter mix and mix gently with a spoon or fork till all the ingredients are well combined into a dough.
  5. Transfer this dough in a plastic bag or container and refrigerate it for at least ½ an hour. At this point, you may leave it in the refrigerator for longer time, till you are ready to bake.
  6. Preheat the oven at 180*C and ready a baking tray with parchment or butter.
  7. Take the cold dough out. Divide it into 24 equal balls.
  8. Take one ball and flatten it gently in your palm. Place on the baking tray. Finish all the dough balls likewise. Place these flattened balls, 2 inches apart from each other. They spread out while baking.
  9. Bake for 10 minutes for soft cookies and 12 minutes for slightly crispier cookies. Do not bake more than that.
  10. Take the tray out of the oven and let the cookies sit for a while, eat after it gets a little cold.

You might find it a little tricky at first but once you get it, you will love it!
