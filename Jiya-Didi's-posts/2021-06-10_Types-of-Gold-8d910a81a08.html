Gold

gold, usually called yellow metal is a precious metal used for making jewelry because of its low reactivity and high ductility and malleability. It is naturally formed in nature and is even used in making spacecrafts as it has a really low reactivity level.

white gold

white gold is famous precious metal. but, unlike gold it does not form naturally. White gold is actually a mixture of about 75% gold and 25 % nickel and zinc. like yellow gold, white gold is also used to make jewelry. White gold and gold both have similar price.

rose gold

Like white gold, rose gold is also not naturally formed. It is also an alloy of gold silver and platinum. it mainly used for making jewelry. It also has similar price that to of the white and yellow gold.
