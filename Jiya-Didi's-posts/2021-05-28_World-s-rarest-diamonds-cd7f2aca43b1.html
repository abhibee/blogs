World’s rarest diamonds

Diamonds are precious stones which are naturally formed. They are cut and polished to enhance its shine. The ones who cut diamonds are highly skilled people. Many diamond merchants have a keen eye for the quality of diamonds . Diamonds has been there for ages and will stay forever. The history of Kohinoor, one of the most famous diamonds has been told many times showing how precious are diamonds.

Diamonds even naturally can come in many different colours and some of them are:

Yellow diamonds

Yellow diamonds have the highest demand in the market, owing to its beautiful colour and less amount in comparison with the other naturally coloured diamonds. Even yellow diamonds have lots of shades. The fancy deep yellow fetches the most amount in the yellow category, due to its beautiful colour.

Pink diamonds

Pink diamonds are one of the most popular diamond colours due to its subtle and vivid colours. Pink diamonds have a high demand but due to its scarcity, it costs much more than yellow diamond. People to get the diamonds sometimes pre-book for it even before the diamond arrives. Like yellow, there are many hues of pink. The most expensive is the Fancy deep pink.

Orange

Orange colour diamonds are extremely rare and very beautiful but, as they have a low demand in market it is not that famous. One well known orange diamond is The Pumpkin orange.

Green

Unlike other colours, it is almost impossible to identify if the colour of the diamond has been artificially enhanced. Therefore, most cutters leave the diamonds untouched and place in jewellery as it is . One famous green diamond is the The Oceans dream.

Blue Diamonds

Blue diamonds are considered the second rarest diamond in the whole world. Blue diamonds are also often connected to royalty because finding one is extremely rare. The most famous blue diamond is The hope diamond.

Red diamonds

Red diamonds by far, are the rarest diamonds to be found on earth. They are not found often and come from very few mines in the world. There is known to be only twenty to thirty red diamonds to be ever found. The most famous red diamond is known to be The Moussaieff Red.
