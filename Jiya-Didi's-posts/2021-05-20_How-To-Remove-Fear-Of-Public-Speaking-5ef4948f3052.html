How To Remove Fear Of Public Speaking

Sometimes the thought of speaking in front of an audience can make people nervous. This is very common and there is no need to worry as there are many ways to cope with it.

Some ways that helped me to remove my fear of public speaking are:

Practice:

I know this might sound familiar but a major part of being confident on stage is being prepared and know in your heart that you have practiced with all your effort. The more you practice the more confident you get. After you feel ready, say it in front of the mirror, this which really boosts the morale as you get to know which areas need further improvement and which areas are your USP.

Don’t over think:

Most of the times even experienced people lose their confidence if they over-think about going on the stage and what would happen if they mess up. The simplest method to prevent is to take deep breaths and remember all the practice you have done. One thing that really helps is visualizing your success, I know this might sound unexpected but this really works! It gives you more motivation to achieve your goal.

Be yourself:

Most of the time the audience like the speakers to show their true selves, as only then one can establish a connect with the audience. Being yourself teaches you to except yourself with your faults as nobody is perfect and this is the key to success in all the fields. You can grow only when you know what are your weaknesses and how can you improve them.

Time and Experience:

Experience matters and the right time is the key. One must not wait for the right time and start as soon as the opportunity first knocks. One might fail the first time but then failure is the best teacher and teaches what not to do the next time. With experience the time is always right. A person learns with experience and improves with time.

Even professional speakers sometimes get nervous on stage, so there is no need to worry and always remember to have fun when you are in front of an audience.
