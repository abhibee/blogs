Essential Information

Author: Dan Brown

Title: The Deception Point

Age rating: 13+

Year of publishing: 2001

Genre: mystery, thriller

Basic Plot

Rachel Sexton is an intelligence analyst. She is the daughter of a selfish senator who is seemingly the president’s most remorseless opponent for the upcoming election. To her surprise Rachel is summoned by the president of the united states himself, to verify a discovery which NASA claims to be the most beguiling discovery the world has ever seen. After a series of exhausting and unforeseen travels, Rachel finds herself in the last place she could ever imagine! Accompanied by Michael Tolland, a marine biologist; Rachel uncovers dark lies of scientific trickery. Fleeing for their lives after failed attempts of assassination. Rachel’s only hope for survival is to find the mastermind of the deceptive ploy and uncover the truth to the world.

Praise and Critique

The story is extremely engrossing and its vivid language does not fail to catch the readers attention. The first chapter leaves a huge plot hole which at first confuses the reader but it’s significance is explained later. The thriller is beautifully put and it gets the reader’s undivided attention throughout. There are some places which makes less sense to some readers but overall, I think it’s a great book to read for all thriller and mystery lovers. After covering all parts i think overall the book deserves atleast4 stars.

By Sukriti Nath on May 19, 2021.
