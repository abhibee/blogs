Pooh bear and the stormy day

It was a stormy day in the hundred acre wood Pooh bear couldn’t go out it was so stormy.

Pooh was delighted to see frog. Frog is Pooh’s friend. Frog could live in the rain.

Pooh thought how delighting it be to live in the rain.

And then Pooh bear friend Lizard came.

Pooh again thought how delighting it be to live in the rain.

And they danced in till the rain stopped.

the end
