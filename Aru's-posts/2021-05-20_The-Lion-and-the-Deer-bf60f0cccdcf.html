The Lion and the Deer

One day a deer was passing by just then a female lion tried to attack it but he missed but the deer did not run away he said that he got hurt and said please don’t eat me the lion agreed. Then he went walking happily then he found a dead bull then he got an idea he pushed the dead bull till the female lion the deer said you can eat this dead bull instead of me then the lion ate the bull and the deer ran away and he did not get hurt and the deer happily grazed the end
