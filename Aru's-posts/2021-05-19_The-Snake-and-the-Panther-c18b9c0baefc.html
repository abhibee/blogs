The Snake and the Panther

One day a snake was wandering around and then suddenly a buffalo jumped out of the bushes and it almost stamped me and a panther was following it. The Panther was hunting it I stoped it and the buffalo ran away and the panther was very angry at the snake because he missed lunch but the snake told that he will give him a bigger buffalo the panther calmed down. The snake had a plan he had a friend bear he was a stone carver. He told him everything and then told him to carve one big boulder into a big buffalo and he agreed and then he showed him the stone he took a bite then his teeth broke and he died at the end
