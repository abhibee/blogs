The crow and the eagle

Once there lived a crow suddenly a eagle came the crow was scared by the eagle. So he flew and flew but the eagle did not give up. And the eagle said why are you flying away from me? I just want to go to U.S.A. I don’t eat or kill crows! I kill or eat fish and the tastiest fishes are found in U.S.A. Can I come too. said the crow. Sure! Why not! Said the eagle. So they went to U.S.A. And they hunted fish and when they cote the fish they taught that the fish was a little heavy and then they hurd a snap and they died because the fish was not a fish and a it was a shark!
