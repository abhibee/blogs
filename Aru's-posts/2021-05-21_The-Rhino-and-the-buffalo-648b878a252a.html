The Rhino and the buffalo

  • Once there lived a buffalo he was eating some grass and then he felt he was eating much faster and then he saw a rhino eating with him.Why are you eating with me Said the buffalo. I can’t eat anywhere because the lions are attacking everywhere but not here! Should we shift from this Savannah? Said the Buffalo. Okay said the rhino. So they walked and they walked till another Savannah. And there were lots of wildebeest and zebras. Suddenly a lion jumped out of the bushes and then the Buffalo charged the lion and the lion died and it all enjoyed and grazed all day the end
