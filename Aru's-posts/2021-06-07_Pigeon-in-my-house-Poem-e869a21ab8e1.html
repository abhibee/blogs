Pigeon in my house!Poem

A pigeon in my house he came from the south region. He was wearing a coat in a vote. He voted the parrot with a carrot in my house! And the parrot was with a carrot. With the pigeon voting, and the parrot was the king which was a very big thing.

By Aryaman Sharma on June 7, 2021.
