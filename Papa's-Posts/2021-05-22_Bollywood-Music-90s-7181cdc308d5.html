Bollywood Music 90s

90s was when some interesting Bollywood movies and music was created. Planning to create a series of posts to examine the music of top grossing movies of the 1990s purely based on what I observed (or lack of it) from my experience. This one is for 1990. Just also checking the recall value and impression then .. since I was about 12 then.

The top-grossing films at the Indian Box Office in 1990 according to WikiPedia
