Communication or the lack of it

George Bernard Shaw said, “The single biggest problem with communication is the illusion that it has taken place.”

We assume a lot of things. Assumptions that we know are true until it has been asserted by other person. It’s always good to have an open conversation to make the maximum out of human interaction. In most cases, people have no negative intentions for each other. They just look for achieving their goals. Misunderstandings spring from the fact above. 


Mostly that we understand well what the other person wants and also that the other person understands well what we want. However in most cases that is not true. 

One interesting version of transaction analysis is the “drama triangle”.
