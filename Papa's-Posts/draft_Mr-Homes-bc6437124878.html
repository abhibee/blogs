Mr Homes

Dr. Brook had taken the horse carriage to the Fenchurch Street train station and reached just in time to catch his train at 7 PM. It was relatively cold for an October evening 1878. His wollen english suit and hat were keeping him warm but he took a mental note to get his leather gloves the next time. The journey back home was about 3 hours. There were not many passengers waiting when the train came to screeching halt at the station. Dr Brook embarked and found his reserved seat next to the window. 

The dim lights inside the train hindered his plan to read his newspaper on the way back.
